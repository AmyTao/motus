# Research Skill

When asked to research a topic, follow this structured approach:

1. **Clarify scope** - Identify what specific aspects of the topic to investigate.
2. **Search** - Use available search/file tools to gather information.
3. **Synthesize** - Combine findings into a clear, structured summary.
4. **Cite sources** - Reference where each piece of information came from.

## Output format

Present findings as:
- A one-paragraph executive summary
- Key findings as bullet points
- Open questions or areas needing further investigation
